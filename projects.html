Link Up

A Social Discovery App

Link Up is a social discovery app where users can find and interact with other users depending on a radius that they set. The goal is that other users are found, the main user can interact with others by "linking up" with them either on the app itself or though other platforms the target user provides. Currently this app is still in the design process but will eventually be a mobile app.

Keyboardless Hospital: Alexa Pre-screening Survey

Proof-of-Concept Alexa Skill

For my Senior Capstone Project, I worked with 5 other students to create a proof-of-concept Alexa Skill for our project's sponsor, the SAP Innovation Center in Newport Beach. The purpose of this skill is to make data entry and patient intake processes for medical personnel more efficient by allowing patients to input answers to pre-screening surveys through a natural conversation with a voice interface. In the long run, SAP hopes to create a "keyboardless" hospital in order to increase patient-doctor interaction and even decrease the interaction with unsanitary surfaces such as keyboards and mice.

For this project, I designed the voice interaction as well as the graphical user interface of the device. Additionally, I improved the natural conversation flow with the Alexa skill by implementing a "continue" feature that allows users to continue a survey after pausing.

Technologies Used: Adobe XD, Amazon Web Services (Cloudwatch, DynamoDB), Alexa SDK, NodeJS

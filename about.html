About Me

Hi! I’m Josh Dela Cruz and I'm a software engineer based in the Bay Area. I recently got my Bachelor’s Degree in Informatics from the University of California, Irvine and I'm now looking for entry-level positions in Software Engineering. Currently, I’m working on building BayWav, a platform allowing creatives in the Bay Area to network with other creatives. Before that, I did Customer Lifecycle Management for Vusar, a startup whose augemented reality app allows design consumers to intelligently interact with their product designs.

I’m interested in tech because I love learning and creating. Growing up in a low-income community in Oakland helped me realize that I want to use technology as a way to empower people coming from similar backgrounds as me. I want to use my love for technology to inspire others to follow their passions.

When I’m not coding or coming up with app ideas, I usually watch anime, hangout with my friends or indulge in social media. Music also has a special place in my heart. I make beats and mash ups on my laptop and I love finding new music to listen to. I even hosted a radio show on UC Irvine’s college radio station!

If you want to get in touch with me, feel free to shoot me a message through email or my socials below!

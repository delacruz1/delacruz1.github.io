About Me

Hi there! I’m Josh Dela Cruz and I'm a software engineer based in the Bay Area. I got my Bachelor’s Degree in Informatics from the University of California, Irvine in 2019 and I'm looking positions in Full Stack Development. Currently, I'm an Associate Software Engineer at Infosys, where I completed trainings in full stack development using Spring and Angular. In the past, I've worked at Vusar, an augmented reality startup in Irvine aiming to streamline 3D design collaboration and visualization, as a Software Engineering and Customer Lifecycle Management Intern.

I’m interested in tech because I love learning and creating. Growing up in a low-income community in Oakland helped me realize that I want to use technology as a way to empower people coming from similar backgrounds as me. I want to use my love for technology to inspire others to follow their passions.

When I'm not working, I'm usually hanging out with friends (virtually, for now), listening to/making music, browsing social media, reading books or playing video games. Recently, I've been getting into Pokemon card collecting so I've been spending some time opening packs :) .

If you want to get in touch with me, feel free to shoot me a message through email or my socials below! Looking forward to connecting with you!
